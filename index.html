Henrique Laurino dos Santos
Marketing Doctoral Student

I am a fifth year Ph.D. student at the Marketing Department of the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania. Currently, I expect to go on the job market in 2024.


My research currently focuses on designing efficient individualized marketing policies - in general through estimating enduring residual effects of previous marketing efforts and network shocks. I am also interested in benchmarking momentum-generation in new product releases, particularly in terms of word-of-mouth.


My methodological interests tend to fall in the realm of stochastic processes, often with support machinery from deep learning and Bayesian statistics
